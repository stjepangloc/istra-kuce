The Property

Space, comfort & Istrian character

Paradise Villa Istria is a generously proportioned family home set within the Paradise Villas resort, 15 km from the Roman amphitheatre of Pula. Four queen bedrooms, two bathrooms, air conditioning throughout.

Step outside to 750 m² of private garden, a 32 m² heated pool, stone barbecue, and shared tennis and basketball courts.
