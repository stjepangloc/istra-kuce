The Property

Contemporary luxury meets
Istrian heritage

Villa Casa Nova is a brand-new luxury retreat in the heart of Bale — a medieval hilltop town in central Istria. Authentic Istrian stone outside, modern design within.

Seven en-suite double bedrooms, a basement spa with sauna and jacuzzi, a game room with mini-cinema and PS5, outdoor summer kitchen, and a 30 m² heated pool.
